Understanding the Impact of Financial Literacy

Statistics on Financial Literacy and Its Impact

Explore the latest data on financial literacy around the globe and in India. These statistics demonstrate the importance of financial education in improving individual financial well-being and reducing negative outcomes such as financial stress and mental health issues like suicides.

Financial stress affects millions of people globally, with a significant portion in India. This stress has detrimental effects, including the rise in suicide rates in regions where financial literacy is low. By focusing on education, we can help mitigate these challenges.

It is essential to highlight the socioeconomic disparities, as the impact of financial stress is disproportionately higher in underprivileged communities. Hence, promoting financial literacy is critical to improving not only financial stability but also mental health and community well-being.
